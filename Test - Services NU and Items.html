Please complete this form with your short-acting, long-acting and permanent service consultations; numbers of clients who are First Time Users of contraception; as well as items provided. 

The form has been designed to demonstrate the relationships between a) consultations and items provided, b) First Time Users and items provided, and c) First Time Users and consultations.  If any of the numbers that are calculated at the end of this form exceed 100, it indicates that there is a problem with the data entered. 

  1. Because First Time Users must each receive at least one Item to count as a First Time User, the FTU as a % of items column can never exceed 100%. This would indicate that clients are being recorded as FTU without having received a modern method of contraception. 
  2. Each First Time User of modern contraception, should also receive a consultation on their chosen method. FTU as a % of consultations should therefore never exceed 100. This would indicate clients being recorded without receiving a modern method of contraception. 
  3. For contraceptive Items that have a one to one relationship to clients, i.e each client receives one item only (IUD, Implants, VSC). Items provided should not exceed consultations. Again, this would indicate items being provided at MA owned clinics without consultations
